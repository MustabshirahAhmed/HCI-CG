  • Norman’s model explains user thinking and feedback.
  • Gulf of Execution = user doesn’t know how to act.
  • Gulf of Evaluation = user doesn’t know what happened.
  • GOMS explains expert task behavior.
  • KLM predicts task completion time.
  • GOMS and KLM ignore feedback and errors.
  • All three together give a full usability view.

Reference: Dix et al., HCI, 3rd ed., Chapters 9–12, pp. 304–400
